Add LinkDrop to iOS share sheet via Shortcuts

iOS doesn't support Web Share Target for PWAs. Use the Shortcuts app to add LinkDrop to your share sheet in 60 seconds:
  1. 1 Open the Shortcuts app → tap + to create a new shortcut
  2. 2 Tap the ⚙️ Settings icon (top right) → enable "Show in Share Sheet" → set Receive to URLs
  3. 3 Tap "Add Action" → search for "Open URLs" → select it
  4. 4 In the URL field tap "URL" → delete it → tap the variable icon (⊕) → pick "Shortcut Input"
  5. 5 Before the variable, type your app URL:
    https://your-linkdrop-url.vercel.app/share-target?url=
  6. 6 Name it "Save to LinkDrop" → tap Done ✅
🎉 Now when you share any link from Safari, YouTube, Instagram etc — tap Save to LinkDrop in the share sheet and the app opens with the URL pre-filled.
